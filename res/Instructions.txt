- Use the arrow keys to move the Robot Up, Left, and Right.
- Click on a block to mine it. The robot must be within 75 pixels of it.
- Spacebar to anchor the robot at current Y level.
- Ctrl + R to Reset entire game (Requires confirmation)
- Ctrl + S to save (Autosaves when focus is lost, window is closed, or layer is unloaded)
- I to open/close your inventory
- T to enable touch mode, click and hold towards the side to move the robot
